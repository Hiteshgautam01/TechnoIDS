ULtraFAce331
ULtraFAce331 series face recognition terminal adopts deep learning algorithm, which helps to recognize the face faster with higher accuracy. It also supports multiple authentication modes: face authentication, etc. When installed on the turnstile, it can be applied in multiple scenarios, such as buildings, enterprises,financial industries, and other important areas.
